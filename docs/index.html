AccelLearn

Privacy Policy

Overview

AccelLearn ("the App") is a spaced-repetition task manager that helps you retain what you learn. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you enter (task titles, URLs, memos, and review results) is stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage and never leaves your device.

No Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. We do not track your behavior, location, or usage patterns.

No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party data services. External URLs you choose to open are handled by your device's default browser; we have no visibility into that activity.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the App from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date below. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions? Please open an issue at github.com/takuaki-taku/AccelLearn.
